FAQ

FAQ

Is commercial use available?
Yes. Use within the scope communicated at the time of inquiry is the standard. For secondary use such as merchandise or product licensing, an additional fee applies. Please specify the intended use when contacting us.
Who holds the copyright?
All copyrights and moral rights remain with Sainyan. Copyright transfer is not available. Clients receive usage rights for the delivered files.
How many revisions are included?
Up to 2 revisions are available during the rough and sketch stages. Revisions after the line art stage are generally not accepted. Minor adjustments may be possible — please feel free to ask. Major changes will be quoted separately.
What payment methods are available?
For individual clients: PayPal, full payment in advance. For corporate clients: PayPal is recommended; invoice payment (NET30 — due within 30 days of invoice date) is also available. Please contact us for details.
Will my commission be made public?
If you prefer non-disclosure, please let us know at the time of inquiry. Completed works may be featured in our portfolio unless otherwise requested.
How long does it take from inquiry to delivery?
Delivery time varies by project, but typically takes around 2–4 weeks. Depending on the complexity and current workload, it may take over a month in some cases. Rush orders are welcome where possible, though a rush fee may apply depending on the schedule.
